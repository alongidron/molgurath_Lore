# Isyra

**Age:** Unknown    
**Size:** 1.80     
**Magic:** ice, fire, lightning     
**Source of Magic:** Isyra bound her life to her castle, turning it into a living mana pool. As long as the castle stands, her mana will never run out. If she dies, the castle is destroyed with her.
She also draws power from her bonded companions, gaining strength through her pets as well.
